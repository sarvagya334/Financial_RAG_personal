| Attribute                          | Details                                                                                                                                                                           |
|------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| What is NSC?                       | Government-backed fixed-income investment scheme offered through Post Offices (and banks in some cases). Investors earn fixed interest, compounded annually, payable at maturity. |
| Current Interest Rate              | 7.7% p.a. for FY 2025-26 (Q2). Interest is compounded annually and paid on maturity.                                                                                              |
| Historical Interest Rates (Recent) | See separate Interest History Table below.                                                                                                                                        |
| Lock-in / Maturity Period          | 5 years (fixed).                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Minimum Investment                 | ₹1,000 (multiples of ₹100).                                                                                                                                                       |
| Maximum Investment                 | No maximum limit (no upper cap).                                                                                                                                                  |
| Tax Benefits (Section 80C)         | Deduction up to ₹1.5 lakh per year under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.                                                                                                       |
| Interest Taxation                  | Interest earned is taxable, but up to the 4th-year interest can be claimed under 80C because it is reinvested; 5th-year interest is fully taxable.                                |
| TDS on NSC                         | No TDS is deducted on maturity.                                                                                                                                                   |
| Loan Facility                      | Can be used as collateral for loans at banks/NBFCs.                                                                                                                               |
| Nomination Facility                | Allowed — can nominate beneficiary(ies).                                                                                                                                          |
| Eligibility                        | Indian resident individuals (incl. minors via guardian). Non-resident Indians cannot subscribe to new NSCs.                                                                       |
| Premature Withdrawal               | Generally not allowed, except on death or court order. Interest payable depends on time of withdrawal.                                                                            |
| Where to Buy                       | Any Post Office branch or some authorized banks; electronic & passbook modes available.                                                                                           |
