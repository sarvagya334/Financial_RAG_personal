| POLICIES                                   |                                                                                                                                                      |
| Category                                   | Provision / Rule                                                                                                                                     |
| Scheme Name                                | Sukanya Samriddhi Account Scheme, 2019                                                                                                               |
| Legal Authority                            | Section 3A, Government Savings Promotion Act, 1873                                                                                                   |
| Notification                               | G.S.R. 914(E)                                                                                                                                        |
| Commencement                               | Date of publication in the Official Gazette                                                                                                          |
| Applicable Rules                           | Government Savings Promotion General Rules, 2018                                                                                                     |
| Definition of Financial Year               | 1 April to 31 March                                                                                                                                  |
| Account Holder                             | Girl child in whose name the account is held                                                                                                         |
| Eligibility (Age at Opening)               | Girl child below 10 years as on date of opening                                                                                                      |
| Who May Open Account                       | One of the guardians of the girl child                                                                                                               |
| Number of Accounts per Girl Child          | Only one account                                                                                                                                     |
| Accounts per Family                        | Maximum two girl children per family                                                                                                                 |
| Exception (Twins/Triplets)                 | More than two accounts permitted if multiple girl children are born in first or second order of birth, supported by affidavit and birth certificates |
| Restriction on Second Order Exception      | Not applicable if first order results in two or more surviving girl children                                                                         |
| Documents Required                         | Birth certificate of girl child and documents of guardian                                                                                            |
| Initial Deposit (Minimum)                  | ₹250                                                                                                                                                 |
| Subsequent Deposit Denomination            | Multiples of ₹50                                                                                                                                     |
| Minimum Annual Deposit                     | ₹250 per financial year                                                                                                                              |
| Maximum Annual Deposit                     | ₹1,50,000 per financial year                                                                                                                         |
| Excess Deposit Handling                    | Excess over ₹1,50,000 earns no interest and is refunded immediately                                                                                  |
| Deposit Period                             | Deposits permitted for 15 years from date of opening                                                                                                 |
| Default Account Definition                 | Failure to deposit minimum annual amount                                                                                                             |
| Regularisation of Default Account          | Allowed up to 15 years from opening on payment of ₹50 per default year + minimum annual deposit                                                      |
| Interest on Unregularised Default Account  | Entire balance earns interest at Scheme rate till closure                                                                                            |
| Interest Rate (12-12-2019 to 31-03-2020)   | 8.4% per annum                                                                                                                                       |
| Interest Rate (on or after 01-04-2020)     | 7.6% per annum                                                                                                                                       |
| Interest Calculation Basis                 | Lowest balance between close of 5th day and end of month                                                                                             |
| Interest Credit Frequency                  | Annually at end of financial year                                                                                                                    |
| Interest Rounding Rule                     | ≥50 paise rounded up; <50 paise ignored                                                                                                              |
| Effect of Account Transfer                 | Interest credited irrespective of change of account office                                                                                           |
| Operation of Account (Below 18 years)      | Operated by guardian                                                                                                                                 |
| Operation of Account (After 18 years)      | Operated by account holder herself                                                                                                                   |
| Premature Closure on Death                 | Immediate closure; balance + interest till date of death paid to guardian                                                                            |
| Interest After Death                       | Payable at Post Office Savings Account rate till closure                                                                                             |
| Premature Closure on Compassionate Grounds | Allowed for life-threatening disease of account holder or death of guardian                                                                          |
| Restriction on Compassionate Closure       | Not permitted before completion of 5 years from opening                                                                                              |
| Withdrawal Eligibility                     | For education purposes                                                                                                                               |
| Maximum Withdrawal Amount                  | Up to 50% of balance at end of preceding financial year                                                                                              |
| Age / Education Condition for Withdrawal   | After attaining 18 years or passing 10th standard, whichever is earlier                                                                              |
| Documents for Withdrawal                   | Confirmed admission offer or fee slip from educational institution                                                                                   |
| Withdrawal Mode                            | Lump sum or instalments                                                                                                                              |
| Withdrawal Frequency                       | Maximum one withdrawal per year                                                                                                                      |
| Maximum Withdrawal Duration                | Up to 5 years                                                                                                                                        |
| Restriction on Withdrawal Amount           | Limited to actual educational requirement                                                                                                            |
| Maturity Period                            | 21 years from date of opening                                                                                                                        |
| Pre-maturity Closure for Marriage          | Permitted on intended marriage                                                                                                                       |
| Marriage Closure Conditions                | Account holder must be at least 18 years                                                                                                             |
| Marriage Closure Time Window               | From 1 month before to 3 months after marriage                                                                                                       |
| Payment on Maturity / Closure              | Balance + interest payable on application (Form-4)                                                                                                   |
| Forms Used                                 | Form-1 (opening), Form-2 (premature closure on death), Form-3 (withdrawal), Form-4 (closure on maturity/marriage)                                    |
| Application of General Rules               | Applicable where Scheme is silent                                                                                                                    |
| Power to Relax                             | Central Government may relax provisions to avoid hardship                                                                                            |
